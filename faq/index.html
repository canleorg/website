Frequently Asked Questions

GENERAL
1. What is Canlé and what kind of events do you organize?

Canlé is a youth-led platform focused on soft skills, global exposure, and community building. We organize events such as Model United Nations (MUNs), diplomatic simulations, cultural exchanges, career-oriented workshops, and networking mixers.

2. What’s the purpose of your MUN or diplomatic events?

Our events aim to foster public speaking, negotiation, leadership, and global awareness by simulating real-world diplomatic scenarios. They also serve as safe spaces for youth to collaborate, ideate, and grow.

3. Do I need any prior experience to attend a Canlé event?

Not at all! Most of our events are beginner-friendly. Whether it’s your first MUN or your tenth, we offer support like training guides, webinars, and mentorship.


MUN / DIPLOMATIC EVENTS
4. How do I register for the MUN or diplomatic event?

You can register through the official Google Form or website link shared on our social media. Early registration is recommended as slots are limited.

5. What are the committees and agendas?

Committees and agendas are announced prior to the event and are carefully curated around current global issues. Keep an eye on our social media and website for updates.

6. Will I get a certificate for participation?

Yes, all active participants receive certificates. High-performing delegates may also be awarded Certificates of Excellence and other recognitions.

7. What is the dress code?

Formal Western attire is usually expected. Some events may have themed dress days (e.g., traditional wear reflecting country colors)—details will be shared in advance.

8. Can I choose my country or committee?

Yes, there is typically a preference form where you can select your top committee/country choices. Allotments are made on a first-come-first-serve basis and delegate strength per committee.


NETWORKING EVENTS
9. What happens at a Canlé networking event?

Our networking mixers are thoughtfully curated spaces to build connections, share ideas, and explore potential collaborations. Activities may include speed networking, theme-based discussion circles, and interactive games.

10. Do I need to prepare anything in advance?

Not necessarily, but it's always a good idea to bring a curious mind, a few questions, and if applicable, your LinkedIn/resume if it’s a career-oriented session.

11. Are these events limited to students from specific institutions?

No. Our events are open to university youth from all disciplines and locations unless specified otherwise.


SUPPORT
12. Is accommodation provided for outstation participants?

Depending on the event scale and partnerships, we may offer accommodation support or help connect you to nearby options. Check the specific event brochure for details.

13. Is food provided during the event?

Yes, our major events typically include lunch and high-tea. This will be mentioned clearly in the event schedule or delegate kit.

14. Who do I contact in case of questions?

You can reach out to us at canle.org@gmail.com or contact@thecanle.com DM us on Instagram canle.official.
