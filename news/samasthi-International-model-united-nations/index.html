Hosted By The Diplomatic Club
In Collaboration With CANLÉ
Academic Partner SGT University

Samashti: International Model United Nations 2025 is set to be one of the most dynamic and transformative conferences of the year. Bringing together bright and passionate delegates from across the globe, this three-day event is more than just a debate forum — it is a platform to exchange ideas, challenge perspectives, and explore solutions to some of the world’s most pressing issues. Through thought-provoking committee sessions and intellectually charged discussions, participants will sharpen their diplomacy, negotiation, and leadership skills while experiencing the true spirit of the United Nations.

Hosted by The Diplomatic Club, Samashti MUN 2025 stands as a celebration of collaboration, cultural exchange, and youth empowerment. With meticulously designed agendas and carefully curated committees, the conference provides an environment where delegates can not only learn international diplomacy but also apply it in real-time simulations. The Diplomatic Club brings its expertise in organizing impactful events to ensure an enriching experience that is as professional as it is memorable.

The conference is held in collaboration with CANLÉ and proudly supported by SGT University as the Academic Partner. This partnership ensures that participants benefit from both academic rigor and global exposure, creating a balance between intellectual depth and practical skill-building. From mentoring sessions to academic support, these collaborations contribute to making Samashti MUN 2025 a holistic experience that transcends conventional debating competitions.

Mark your calendars for 14–16 November 2025, as Samashti MUN promises not just debate and diplomacy, but also vibrant cultural activities, interactive stalls, and exciting opportunities for networking. Delegates will leave the conference with lifelong friendships, enhanced confidence, and a deeper understanding of international relations. Whether you are a seasoned MUN enthusiast or a first-time participant, Samashti MUN 2025 offers an unforgettable journey of discovery, diplomacy, and dialogue.

Committee Capacity (Pax) Agenda

SOCHUM 120 Mental health in adolescents: global strategies for access to support, de-stigmatization, school/internet interventions.
ECOSOC 150 Sustainable urbanization: affordable housing, green transport & reducing pollution in rapidly growing cities.
UNGA – Sixth Committee (Legal) 170 Legal challenges in regulating artificial intelligence: liability, accountability, cross-border data protection.
UNSC 40 Responding to piracy, illegal arms trade, and territorial disputes in strategic waterways.
